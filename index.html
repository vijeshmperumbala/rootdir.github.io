Our Mission

To print and publish books, pamphlets, news papers, etc., useful to the growth of the society and to create growth in social and cultural fields. To acquire properties in order to attain necessary financial security to achieve the goal of the Trust, helpful to the welfare of the society. To provide opportunities to the destitute to regain their lost own personality since the humanitarian activities are sacred one. To create fearless atmosphere to the destitute and orphans. To establish schools, educational institutions to study for the children, aged persons and to start other activities to that effect. To help and identify the students who face difficulty to continue the education due to the financial difficulty and to maintain good standard in higher studies.

Our Vision

To provide assistance to rehabilitate the helpless, distress, aged neglected persons in the society. To eradicate the educational backwardness of the children of the economically backward sections, in order to enhance the educational facilities, establish necessary schools, colleges, technical institutions, free training centers, etc, and conducting awareness. To identify their hereditary employment and performance and creating necessary opportunities to foster and training them. Attempting to preserve humaneness, brotherhood, love and harmony between the people in the society through service. To unite the persons through service, living in different level and different areas of the society and to conduct awareness against the caste, communal, beastly act. To rehabilitate the orphan children, destitute persons in the society and rendering food, cloth, treatment to such persons.
